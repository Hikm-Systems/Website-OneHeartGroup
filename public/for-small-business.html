FAQs

What is Ripple AP automation?
Ripple AP automation is a comprehensive accounts payable solution seamlessly integrated into the Ripple platform. It streamlines the entire process of bill management, automating everything from bill entries to approvals and payments, while maintaining robust financial controls to ensure accuracy and compliance.
What payment options are available in Ripple?
Ripple supports multiple payment methods, including ACH, Same-Day ACH, Card, Check, and international wire transfers (in both USD and foreign currencies). This flexibility allows you to manage all your payments directly within the Ripple platform.
How does Ripple handle check payments?
When a bill is approved for payment, Ripple will automatically debit the connected bank account on the scheduled payment date. Ripple then issues a check on your behalf, ensuring timely and accurate payments to your vendors.
How can I extend payment terms on my bills?
With Ripple Flex, you can easily extend payment terms to 30, 60, or 90 days. This feature provides access to interest-free capital, helping you manage cash flow more effectively without worrying about hidden fees or penalties.
Can I set up recurring bills?
Yes, Ripple allows you to easily set up recurring bills directly within the platform. Customize the payment frequency and specify an ending date, and Ripple will automatically handle these routine vendor payments for you, ensuring they are managed efficiently and on time.
