About us

Ripple is for business builders

At Ripple, we're dedicated to leveling the financial playing field for small and medium-sized businesses.

Empowering businesses with the ultimate growth tool

Ripple was created to address a fundamental gap: many small businesses struggle to access the capital they need to thrive. Our platform empowers these businesses by offering fair, unbiased capital, enabling them to invest in growth, strengthen their communities, and focus on what truly matters—innovation and success.

Ripple goes beyond financing by offering additional tools and solutions tailored to support business growth, from smart underwriting to seamless payment management and spend insights. Our comprehensive approach ensures that businesses have the resources they need to grow efficiently.

Meet our leadership team

Our leadership team is driven by the mission to empower business builders everywhere. With a deep understanding of the challenges faced by entrepreneurs, they guide Ripple in creating solutions that truly make a difference.
